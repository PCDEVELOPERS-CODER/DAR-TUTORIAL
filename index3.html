DAR NOTES:

  • First, log in using your username and password via the "LOGIN" button.
  • Next, you will be redirected to the Selection page.
  • On the Selection page, tap the "DAR" button.
  • Enter the description of activities on the DAR Details page.
  • You can submit DAR details for a specific date only once.
  • Only today and the past 2 days are available for selection.
  • Before entering a description, select your work using the "Select Work" combo box.
  • After filling all 5 work description boxes, press the "Submit" button.
  • The "Submit" button becomes visible only after entering at least one work description.
  • After submitting your details, you will return to the Selection page.
  • On this page, tap the "DAR History" button to view your submission history.
  • Only the Team Leader (TL) can approve your activities.
  • To submit DAR for another date, press the "ADD" button.
  • Approved DAR entries appear in green; unapproved ones appear in light red.
  • Press the "Back" button to return from the DAR History page.
  • Press the "EXIT" button to log out from the app.

ATTENDANCE NOTES:

  • First, log in using your username and password via the "LOGIN" button.
  • Next, you will be redirected to the Selection page.
  • On the Selection page, tap the "ATTENDANCE" button.
  • On the Attendance page, take a picture of yourself.
  • The "Submit" button will not work unless you take a picture.
  • After submitting your picture, you will return to the Selection page.
  • Tap the "Attendance History" button to view your history.
  • Only the TL can approve your attendance picture.
  • Approved entries appear green; unapproved ones are light red.
  • Use "Back" to return from Attendance History.
  • Use "EXIT" to log out.
