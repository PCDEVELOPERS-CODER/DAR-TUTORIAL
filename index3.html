LOGIN PAGE:

  • First, log in using your username and password by clicking the "ENTER" button and turn on your location before login a app

SELECTION PAGE:

  • In the selection screen, there are 4 buttons. Click the "DAR Entry" button to enter your DAR details.
  • Click the "DAR History" button to check and analyze your DAR details.
  • Click the "Attendance Entry" button to submit your attendance.
  • Click the "Attendance History" button to check and analyze your attendance entries.

DAR ENTRY PAGE:

  • On the DAR Entry page, your name and department are displayed by default.
  • Next, select your In Time and Out Time correctly.
  • Then, select the date for your DAR. You can only select today's date or any of the previous 3 days.
  • Only one date can be selected at a time.
  • There are 5 work description fields, each with a corresponding combo box.
  • A description box will be enabled only after selecting your role in the combo box, based on your department.
  • The "Submit" button will appear only if at least one work description field is filled and the "Tomorrow's Program" is also entered.
  • If these conditions are not satisfied, then the "Submit" button will not be visible.

DAR HISTORY PAGE:

  • On DAR history page, to view your DAR submission history.
  • Once you have submitted your DAR for a specific date, it cannot be edited тАФ you can only view the entries.
  • Only the Team Leader, Immediate Head, Incharge, or Manager can approve your activities.
  • To submit a DAR for another date, press the "ADD" button.
  • Approved DAR entries appear in green, while unapproved entries appear in light red.
  • Press the "Back" button to return to the selection page.

ATTENDANCE ENTRY PAGE:

  • On the Attendance Entry page, take a picture of yourself alo there an option to both front and back camera!
  • The "Submit" button will not work unless you take a picture.
  • After submitting your picture, you will return to the Selection page.

ATTENDANCE HISTORY PAGE:

  • On the Attendance history page, to view your attendance submission history.
  • Only the Team Leader, Immediate Head, Incharge, or Manager can approve your attendance.
  • Approved attendance appears in green; unapproved attendance are appears in light red.

CHANGE PASSWORD PAGE:

  • On the Change Password page, enter your registered email ID, your current password, and your new password. Confirm the new password, then click the "Update Password" button.
  • Your password will be successfully updated.
