Notes:

  • First, login with your username and password via "LOGIN" button.
  • Second, enter the description of activities in DAR Details page.
  • You can submit DAR details for a specific date only once.
  • Only today and past 2 days are allowed for selection.
  • Before entering description, select your work using "Select Work" combo box.
  • After filling all 5 work discription boxes, press the "Submit" button.
  • Submit button is visible only after entering at least one work description.
  • Go to the History page to check your submitted activities.
  • Only TL can approve your activities.
  • To submit another dateтАЩs DAR, press "ADD" button.
  • Approved DAR shows green color, unapproved appear in light red color.
  • Press the "EXIT" button to log out from the app.
